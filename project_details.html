Project Description

The Smart City Traffic Management System is an innovative solution designed to enhance urban mobility by integrating IoT devices, advanced algorithms, and real-time analytics. The system offers a range of features to address modern traffic challenges.

  • Real-time traffic monitoring: Achieved through IoT sensors that collect and process live data to identify congestion and traffic patterns.
  • Citizen feedback platform: Ensures that traffic-related concerns are analyzed and addressed effectively.
  • Smart traffic signals: Dynamically adjust their timings based on traffic flow, reducing delays and improving efficiency.
  • Parking solutions: Utilize a Trie data structure to manage and update parking space availability in real time, providing instant updates to users.
  • Emergency vehicle routing: Optimized using a 2-3 Tree, ensuring minimal delays for ambulances and fire trucks.
  • EV charging station management: Supports sustainable practices by managing EV charging stations across the city, ensuring optimal utilization and easy access for electric vehicle users.

By combining cutting-edge technologies, this system improves traffic flow, prioritizes safety, supports eco-friendly initiatives, and enhances the overall urban experience.
